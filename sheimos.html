Sheimos Information

Did you know that back in the day, they used a Gemara for hundreds of years?

When a city would get together once every few years to bury their precious and dear Seforim, the Tzaddikim and the community would come out and escort the Sheimos to burial. It is an Eis Ratzon and a special time to daven: ‘Just as we have pity on Hashem’s precious Torah, so too He should have Rachmanus on us.’

When you give your Sheimos to The Hock Cleveland, you can rest assured that it is buried—and buried properly.

Specifically, the whole process is transparent and pictures and videos are available of the Sheimos being buried under the correct amount of earth, and covered right away without being exposed to the natural elements. There are many other fascinating details that you can reach out to learn more.

This is a very unique service and this process is only guaranteed when you give your Sheimos to The Hock Cleveland to bury.

Nowadays, there is more Sheimos than we know what to do with and we bury Seforim and papers that the previous generations would have wished they would have had. If we can all be more careful to realize that Sheimos isn’t ours; It’s Hashem’s holy Torah. And that we should try to be more mindful of that, hopefully Hashem will count that as a zchus and watch over us.
